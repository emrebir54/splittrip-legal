# Privacy Policy for SplitTrip

Last updated: 2026

SplitTrip does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Usage
All data entered into the app (expenses, trips, participants) is stored locally on your device.

We do not:
- collect personal information
- track users
- use analytics services
- share data with third parties

## Third-Party Services
SplitTrip does not use third-party tracking or advertising services.

## Security
Your data remains on your device. We do not have access to it.

## Changes
We may update this policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
